Projects

The Wait

"The Wait" is a captivating video project that takes viewers on a thrilling narrative journey filled with anticipation, risk, and emotional turmoil. Through skillful storytelling and minimal dialogue, the project immerses the audience in a world of exaggerated emotions and the suspense of waiting. The storyline revolves around a young girl who takes a daring step by sending a risky text message to her crush. Viewers are drawn into her world, experiencing the intense anticipation and anxiety as they eagerly await the response that will shape her hopes and dreams. With each scene, the video masterfully captures the girl's impatience, her constant phone-checking, the panic of a dead battery, and the frantic race to find a charger. The audience shares in her highs and lows, feeling the weight of hope and the sting of disappointment.

Turn Around

“Turn Around” is a soundscape in the form of a thrilling radio drama. This unique project relies on skillfully crafted sounds and minimal dialogues, complemented by a single image as a visual reference. The deliberate intention behind this approach is to empower the audience to engage their imagination, as their own minds become the source of fears and the provider of vivid mental imagery. By inviting active participation in the storytelling process, "Turn Around" offers an immersive experience where the boundaries of narrative are expanded, creating a deeply personalized journey for each listener.

Soldier, Poet, King

"Soldier, Poet, and King: The Interactive Game" is a webcomic that immerses users in a world of three distinct characters. Through an innovative and dynamic experience, the comic introduces the concept of a game revolving around these characters. The overarching theme explores the unique strengths and weaknesses of the soldier, poet, and king. The comic comprises seven panels, each carefully crafted to serve a purpose in the narrative. From the visually captivating cover page showcasing the characters to the interactive elements like the on/off button and character exploration screens, the project utilizes 2D software to create an engaging and interactive web-based experience. By pushing the boundaries of sequential art on a web page, the comic seeks to captivate users and offer them an immersive journey into the world of the soldier, poet, and king.

Website: https://nasaralhumaidi.github.io/NasarAlHumaidi-Comic/

GitHub: https://github.com/NasarAlHumaidi/NasarAlHumaidi-Comic.git

The Appreciation of Art and Nature

"The Appreciation of Art and Nature" explores the inherent connection between art and nature, celebrating their beauty and influence on our daily lives. The overarching concept revolves around intertwining these elements, showcasing how they coexist harmoniously. The chosen theme embraces a lighthearted and artistic approach, highlighting the diverse facets that encompass our existence, from the tactile experience of touching dirt to the awe-inspiring architecture surrounding us. The goal was to create an engaging and interactive experience that captivates viewers, immersing them in the allure of art. Vibrant colors were deliberately selected, including the vivid green symbolizing nature, to enhance visual appeal and evoke a sense of wonder throughout the webpage.
